God is Omniscient, the most ancient One, the Controller, subtler than the subtlest, the Support of all, and the possessor of an inconceivable divine form; He is brighter than the sun, and beyond all darkness of ignorance.  One who at the time of death, with unmoving mind attained by the practice of Yog, fixes theprāṇ(life-airs) between the eyebrows, and steadily remembers the Divine Lord with great devotion, certainly attains Him.
